Fragmentation Does Not Solve Logical Omniscience [B.A. Thesis]

Argues that the proposal by Elga and Rayo (2022) in Fragmentation and Logical Omniscience fails as a solution to the problem of logical omniscience in Bayesian decision theory. Fragmentation leaves too much underdetermined to give any verdicts on rationality, while the metalinguistic strategy misidentifies the source of agents' failures in cases like logical puzzles and mathematical computation.
